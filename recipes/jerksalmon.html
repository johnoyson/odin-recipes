Jerk Salmon Recipe

Description

Jerk salmon is an easy protein to pull together for a quick weeknight meal or party. What I love most about using salmon for this recipe is that the firmness of the fish holds up well to the heavy seasoning and spices of the jerk marinade.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Salmon steaks
  • 2-3 Tablespoon Jerk Seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Brush salmon lightly with the oil, then season with salt and pepper. Followed by the Jerk Spice Seasoning
  2. Preheat a large heavy skillet or grill pan over medium heat for about 2 minutes.
  3. Add salmon to pan; cook salmon for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until desired degree of doneness is reached
